GOA

Places to be Visited

Goa is a state in India on the southwestern coast with a mainland district and an offshore island. It's known for its beaches, temples, churches, festivals, and warm people. Goa is also known as the "Party Capital of India" because of its nightlife, shacks, and laid-back accommodations.

Famous Food

Goan cuisine is mainly based on seafood, with fish and rice as staples. The cuisine also features vinegar, especially toddy vinegar, which is made from coconut sap. Goan cuisine is also influenced by Portuguese cuisine, as seen in the use of vinegar.

Arts & Culture

Goa's culture is a blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures. The state's cultural heritage includes dances, folk songs, visual arts, music, and folk tales.
