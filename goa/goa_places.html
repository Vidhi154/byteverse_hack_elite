GOA

Places you can visit in GOA

Palolem Beach

Palolem Beach is a white sand beach in Canacona, Goa, India, known for its calm waters, nightlife, and stunning sunsets. The beach is lined with palm trees and colorful wooden shacks, and faces Canacona Island, which is known for its resident monkeys.

Panaji

Panaji remains the most famous tourist destination in Goa, in terms of beaches, churches, carnivals and natural beauty. Being the capital city, it is the focal point of tourism in Goa. The town has some beautiful red-blue-green-yellow roofed buildings made in Portuguese Baroque style.

Candolim

Candolim is the first beach that can be approached from the capital city of Panaji. Besides a place of tourist interest, Candolim has historic significance as well. The founder of science of hypnotism, Father Abbe Faria, was born here.

Anjuna Beach

Anjuna Beach is famous for its vibrant nightlife. The beach is dotted with numerous clubs and shacks that host parties and live music performances. The most famous among them is the Curlies Beach Shack, known for its psychedelic parties and trance music.
