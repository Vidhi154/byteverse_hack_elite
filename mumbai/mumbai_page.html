MUMBAI

Places to be Visited

Mumbai is a densely populated city on India’s west coast. A financial center, it's India's largest city. On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic Gateway of India stone arch, built by the British Raj in 1924. The city's also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.

Famous Food

The favorite food in Mumbai is often considered to be street food, particularly dishes like vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri. These dishes are popular among locals and tourists alike and are known for their delicious flavors and diverse ingredients.

Arts & Culture

Mumbai is home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, and the city's distinctive ensemble of Victorian and Art Deco buildings. The seven islands that constitute Mumbai were originally home to communities of Marathi language speaking Koli people.
