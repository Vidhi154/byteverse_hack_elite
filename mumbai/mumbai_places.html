MUMBAI

Places you can visit in MUMBAI

Global Vipassana Pagoda

A warm and inviting spiritual centre, Global Vipassana Pagoda was built to help increase harmony and to acquaint people with concepts of Vipassana and Buddha’s teachings.

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple is a prominent Hindu temple located in Prabhadevi, Mumbai, India. Dedicated to Lord Ganesha, it is one of the most revered and visited temples in the city.

Gateway Of India Mumbai

Located on the waterfront in Colaba, South Mumbai, this arch monument is Mumbai’s prime landmark. It was built by the British to welcome King George V and Queen Mary during a royal visit in 1911.

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a 3.6 km long promenade in Mumbai, India, that runs along the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Road. It's also known as the Queen's Necklace, and is a popular spot for leisurely walks and watching the sunset. The road is banana-shaped, with six lanes, and runs along the coast of a natural bay.
