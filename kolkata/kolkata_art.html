About Kolkata

Known as the ‘City of Joy’, Kolkata holds an important place in the economy, politics and culture of India. The capital of West Bengal, Kolkata is one of the four metropolitan cities in India and has retained its quintessential charm despite modernization.

Earlier known as Calcutta, the city was founded in 1698 and served as the capital of British India till 1911 and then was shifted to Delhi. The city was renamed, Kolkata in 2001. Located along the banks of river Hoogly, Kolkata is connected to Howrah by the iconic Howrah bridge.

Art and Creativity

Kolkata is famous for its wall painting, fancy dolls and many. The Terracotta slabs and metal works are superior in this state. The ornaments with precious stones are the sample for its unique craft work. In this city, most of the things are made up of bamboo.

Cultural Heritage

Cultural diversity, exotic, vibrant, revolutionary heritage etc. are the features that prevail in the culture of Kolkata. Kolkata’s rich culture and artistic thought among the people has made it an appropriate place for cultural-meditation
